Privacy Policy - Last Word Standing

Last Updated: January 3, 2025

Introduction

Last Word Standing ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

  • Game Progress & Statistics: We store your game progress, scores, tickets, crowns, and achievements locally on your device using UserDefaults.
  • Device Information: Basic device information such as device type, operating system version, and app version for compatibility purposes.

Information from Third-Party Services

Google AdMob (Advertising)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in our app. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • Location data (general location)
  • App activity and interactions

For more information about Google AdMob's data practices, visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Apple StoreKit (In-App Purchases)

When you make in-app purchases:

  • Purchase history and transaction information is handled by Apple
  • We do not store payment information
  • Purchase receipts are used solely to validate purchases and award in-game currency

For more information about Apple's privacy practices, visit: https://www.apple.com/privacy/

How We Use Your Information

  • Game Functionality: To save your progress, track achievements, and provide personalized game experiences
  • Advertising: To show relevant advertisements and enable rewarded video ads
  • Analytics: To improve our app, fix bugs, and understand user behavior
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide support

Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Game progress and statistics are stored locally on your device
  • Cloud Storage: Not currently implemented - all data remains on your device
  • Third-Party Services: Ad networks and analytics services may store data according to their own privacy policies

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Ad Networks: Google AdMob receives device and usage information to show relevant ads
  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate our app
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Opt out of personalized ads through your device settings
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
