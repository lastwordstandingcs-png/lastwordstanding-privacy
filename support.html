Common Questions

  • How do I get more tickets?
    You can earn tickets by completing rooms, watching ads, or purchasing ticket packs in the shop.
  • How does Premium work?
    Premium subscribers get unlimited ads, 2x ticket rewards, and access to all premium features.
  • How do I restore purchases?
    Go to the Shop and tap "Restore Purchases" to restore your previous purchases.
  • Where is my game progress?
    Your progress is saved locally on your device. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID.

Report a Bug

If you encounter any bugs or issues, please email us at lastwordstandingcs@gmail.com with:

  • A description of the issue
  • What you were doing when it occurred
  • Your device model and iOS version (if possible)
